🚀 Growth Hacks

Organik o'sish uchun maxfiy taktikalar

👑 Dream 100 Strategy

  • Mahalliy bloggerlar: Termiz/Surxondaryo bloggerlarini aniqlash, ular bilan collaboration, shoutout almashish
  • Maktab direktorlari: Har bir maktab direktori bilan tanishish, ular orqali reklama
  • Universitetlar: SamDU, TermDU student communities bilan partnership
  • Mahalla oqsoqollari: Har bir mahallada bitta "ambassador" topish
  • Chet elda o'qigan talabalar: Ularning success stories'ini ko'rsatish

📢 STEPPS Framework (Jonah Berger)

  • Social Currency: "Men 9.0 teacher'dan o'qiyapman" — bu maqtanadigan narsa
  • Triggers: Har safar "IELTS" eshitsa — "Elites" esiga tushsin
  • Emotion: Dilshod'ning struggle story — ko'z yoshi keladi
  • Public: Elites branded hoodie/t-shirt — ko'chada ko'rinib tursin
  • Practical Value: Har bir reel — foydali tips, SAVE qilishadi
  • Stories: Har bir o'quvchi — o'z success story'si bor

🏘️ Mahalliy Taktikalar

  • Referral Program: Har bir yangi o'quvchi olib kelsa — 1 oy 50% chegirma
  • School Visits: Maktablarga bepul master-class o'tkazish
  • Telegram Groups: Har bir mahallaning Telegram guruhiga kontent tarqatish
  • Local Events: Spelling Bee, English Day, Open Mic tashkil qilish
  • Wall of Fame: Eng yaxshi natijalarni ko'chaga banner qilish
  • Student UGC: O'quvchilarga TikTok challenge berish — viral qilish
