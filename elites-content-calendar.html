📈 Muhim KPI'lar (Oylik maqsadlar)

  • Followers o'sishi: +500-1000 ta/oy
  • Har bir Reels: min 1000 view, 50+ like, 10+ comment
  • DM'lar: kuniga 5-10 ta yangi murojaat
  • Save rate: 3-5% (ta'limiy kontentda 5-8%)
  • Share rate: 2-3% (hazilkash kontentda 5%+)
  • Story engagement: 10-15% (poll/quiz interaction)
  • Conversion: oyiga 15-25 yangi o'quvchi

🏆 Top O'quv Markazlardan O'rganilgan Strategiyalar

  • EIT LC (Samarqand): Consistent posting + IELTS tips = trust building. Ular har kuni 1 ta foydali tip beradi
  • Innovative Centre: Before/After transformations - o'quvchi natijalarini ko'rsatish eng kuchli social proof
  • GEC (Genius English): Reels + Carousel kombinatsiyasi. Carousel = save, Reels = reach
  • BBC Learning English: Micro-learning format - 1 post = 1 bilim. Oddiy, tushunarli, save-worthy
  • YES Education Center: Community building - o'quvchilar o'zlari content yaratadi (UGC)

⏰ Optimal Posting Vaqtlari (O'zbekiston)

  • Eng yaxshi vaqt: 07:00-08:00 (ertalab), 12:00-13:00 (tushlik), 19:00-21:00 (kechqurun)
  • Eng yaxshi kunlar: Seshanba, Chorshanba, Payshanba (eng yuqori engagement)
  • Dushanba: Yangi hafta energiyasi - motivatsion/ta'limiy content
  • Juma: Conversion kuni - sotuv/CTA kuchliroq ishlaydi
  • Weekend: Story only - followerlar relax holatda, engagement suhbat orqali
